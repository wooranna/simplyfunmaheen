About Us


We are actually a robotics team of 8 children. We are on our third year (2020) of taking apart of FLL. We really enjoy it. As an assignment we were to create anything that will benefit someones health.

I (Maheen) decided to create a website that people could do in quarantine or in lockdown because of COVID-19. I do Tae Kwon Do so I decided to create a video on what I am strong at. I also decided to do mindfulness because in tough times like this it is very important to stay calm and mindfull.
